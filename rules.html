LEAGUE RULEBOOK — BLR INDIA COMP RULEBOOK

Rulebook Overview & Council +

NOTE: Only the Council may edit rules. Any rule announced without director confirmation does not apply. Rules apply to pickups, scrims and leagues.

RULES COUNCIL: Alex • Ppninja • Haru • Elite

Match Info — Basic Format +

  • Matches: 5v5.
  • 20 minutes to field 5 players or auto-forfeit.
  • Pickups: BO3.
  • Scrims / League: FT3, sides swap every leg.
  • Indian servers (GMT) only.
  • Players must be signed to teams.
  • Spectators removable on captain request.

PC Check Policy +

If cheating, exploiting, alt usage or multi-teaming is suspected, the player must PC check within 24 hours.

Sportsmanship & Evidence +

  • Toxicity allowed only as light trash talk.
  • Discrimination, racism, homophobia forbidden.
  • Pickups require at least one referee.
  • No-ref matches waive complaints unless clip evidence exists.

Illegal Actions (FULL LIST) +

  • Duplicate styles (GK exception).
  • Mid-leg style/flow swaps.
  • Identical avatars.
  • Hidden flows or cosmetics.
  • Bug abuse / lag switching / holding X.
  • Invisible box steals.
  • Shot-hiding awakenings.
  • Time wasting / stall dribbling.
  • Resetting during play.
  • Illegal flags.
  • TP-shot abuse.

Servers, Goals & Server Swap Rules +

  • Kickoff bug own-goals = Free Goal.
  • Illegal goals give opponent +1.
  • Server swap rules after 2 and 4 legs apply.

Substitution & Disconnect Rules +

  • No subs mid-leg.
  • Max 5 minutes to sub.
  • Disconnect rules for 4v5, 3v5, 4v4 fully apply.
  • GK disconnect must be replaced instantly.
  • Server owner DC rules apply.

Pairing Styles & Flow Rules +

  • Igaguri / Don / Aiku banned together.
  • Sae & Reaper restrictions apply.
  • Blood Moon Rin + NEL Rin banned.
  • Skeleton Nagi + Nagi banned.
  • Only two defensive styles allowed.

Goalkeeper Rules +

  • No steal/deflect as GK.
  • No auto-track passes.
  • Karasu & Don perm banned GK.
  • Duped GK must play styleless.
