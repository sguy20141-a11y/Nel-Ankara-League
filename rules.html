LEAGUE RULEBOOK

All official competitive regulations of Nel Ankara FC. Failure to comply may result in Free Goals, Round Redo, Match Void, Ban, or Forfeit.

Match Format & Server Rules

5v5 Main Game Mode.
Each team must have 5 players within 20 minutes or auto forfeit.

Pickups: BO3.
Scrim/League: FT3.
Sides swapped each leg.

All matches must be played on Indian Servers (GMT).

Player Eligibility & Evidence

Players must be signed to participate in scrims.
Spectators removable unless Substitute / Referee / Streamer.

Pickups require at least 1 referee unless both captains agree otherwise. No complaints without clips.

Sportsmanship & Conduct

Toxic behaviour results in warning → punishment → force disband.
Discrimination of any kind not allowed.
Only dual accounting CF & GK allowed.
Selling intentionally = 1-day ban.

Game Illegal Actions

Duplicate styles (GK must go styleless) → Pause + Free Goal.
Swapping styles mid-leg → Free Goal.
Avatar similarity → Pause + Change.
Bug abuse / lag switch / holding X → Free Goal.
Invisible box entry → Free Goal.
Awakening hide shot (Aiku/Karasu/Reo/Gagamaru) → Free Goal.
Time wasting 15–20 sec → Penalized.
Reset during attack → Free Goal.
Illegal flags → Match void.
TP shot with ability → Not allowed.
1 wall inner box max, 2 defenders max.
No god keybinds during FG.

Substitution & Disconnect Rules

No subs mid-leg.
5 minutes max to sub.
Disconnect → play until goal then pause.
3v5 → 1 temp sub allowed.
GK disconnect → must rejoin immediately.
Frequent disconnects → permanent sub.
PS owner disconnect → redo leg with same score & time.

Pairing & Team Composition

Igaguri/Don/Aiku cannot be paired.
Sae/Reaper Sae + Charles banned.
Blood Moon Rin + NEL Rin banned.
Only 2 defensive styles per team.
Scythe Slam / Off Balance / Lunar Axis not usable in opponent half.

Goalkeeper Rules

No steal/deflect as GK.
No auto-track passes.
Perm banned GK styles: Karasu, Don Lorenzo.
Off-ball moves banned in box.
Autogoal moves banned.
